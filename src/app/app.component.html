Our Mission

Ladders for Learning is dedicated to transforming lives through education in the Haroonabad and Bhawalnagar districts of Punjab, Pakistan. We build schools, train teachers, and provide resources so that every child — regardless of background — has the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential.
